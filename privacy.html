Privacy Policy



Effective date: March 29, 2021

Naofumi Bot Team ("us", "we", or "our") operates the https://naofumi.ml website (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms and Conditions, accessible from https://naofumi.ml/

Information Collection And Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Discord user ID.
  • Discord channel ID.
  • Discord Guild ID.
  • Member count of Discord servers.

Usage Data

We may also collect information how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include types of ("Personal Data") and other diagnostic data.

Tracking

We use Discord Channel Webhooks ("Posting the logs to secured discord channels.") to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information.

Examples of Discord Channel Webhooks we use:

  • Guild Logging. Each time bot joins or leaves a guild ("Discord Server") an embed is posted in a secured channel of our support server, that embeds includes but not limited to: Guild ID, Guild Name, Guild Owner Username, Guild Owner Discriminator, Guild Owner ID and Guild Member Count
  • Command Logging. Each time a command is used ("invoked by a discord user") an message is posted in a secured channel of our support server and storing in a text file, that message includes but not limited to: Message Content, Command's Used Name, Message Author Discriminator, Message Author Username, Message Channel Name, Message Channel ID, Message Guild Name and Message Guild ID

Use of Data

Naofumi Bot Team uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To provide customer care and support
  • To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues
  • To detect, prevent and address abuse cases
  • For the operation of the Bot Systems.

Transfer Of Data

Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including Personal Data, to United States and process it there.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

Naofumi Bot Team will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of your Personal Data will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of your data and other personal information.

Disclosure Of Data

Naofumi Bot Team may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
